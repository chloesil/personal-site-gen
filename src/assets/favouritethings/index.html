Welcome to
An exploration into the meaning of value held in our most prized possessions
You can choose to explore through one of the following two journeys:

1
Memory & Abstraction
This section contains an assortment of objects and their stories. These objects are not displayed in a straightforward way, but visualized through the memories in which they are contained. The words and imagery presented to you is meant to paint a picture of each object as it is experienced by its owner on a daily basis.
2
Logic & Analysis
This section aims to further the abstract theories gleaned from the first section into analyzed data sets which might be useful in answering specific questions raised at the beginning of this project. Using various methods, the stories displayed in the first section will be interpreted and extracted to create visualizations of a more logical format.
